## **Curriculum Outline (12 weeks)**

## **Phase 01: Build the Base (Month 1\)**

**Focus**: B2C Fundamentals, AI-Native Research, and Identity Architecture.

* **Week 1: Foundations & The Identity Shift**  
  * **UX Theory**: Master UCD (User-Centered Design) and the 10 Usability Heuristics for B2C.  
  * **Design process:** Empathize → Define → Ideate → Prototype → Test  
  * **AI Skills**: Master **Prompt Mechanics 101** using Context, Task, and Constraint frameworks.  
  * **Tools**: Setup the **FigJam Workspace** for collaborative rapid ideation and logic mapping.  
  * **Project**: Finalize the Problem Statement and Competitor Audit for the **E-Learning/E-commerce App**.  
  * **Soft skills**  
    * confidence reset: learner → professional  
    * communication basics in product teams

* **Week 2: AI Research & Foundations**  
  * **UX**  
    * User Interviews, surveys, observation  
    * empathy maps, personas, journey maps  
    * Generate AI-assisted Personas and Journey Maps focused on emotional friction.  
  * **AI Research**: Deploy **NotebookLM** to synthesize long-form user interviews into deep insights.  
  * **Freelance Track**: Introduction to the "Independent Consultant" mindset and professional project scoping.  
  * **Systems**: Setup the **Notion Design Hub** for automated task tracking and project management.  
  * **Project**: Document the final Research Synthesis and User Flows within your Notion Wiki.  
  * **Soft skills**  
    * listening, asking better questions  
    * presenting research insights

* **Week 3: Visual Language & Jitter Motion**  
  * **Visual Theory**: Apply Hierarchy via 8pt grids, modular type scales, and color psychology.  
  * **Figma Mastery**: Master **Auto-Layout 5.0** and responsive component architecture for scalability.  
  * **Motion**: Intro to **Jitter**; animate a cinematic "Hero" transition for the app's home screen.  
  * **AI Generation**: Use **Leonardo.ai** to generate custom, brand-consistent avatars and UI imagery.  
  * **Project**: Convert low-fi sketches into high-fidelity UI iterations with established style guides.

* **Week 4: Usability & The Studio Day Trip**  
  * **Usability**: Apply Fitts’s and Hick’s Laws to optimize mobile navigation and CTA placement.  
  * **LOOK Pillar**: Physical presence and professional framing during the **Studio Day Trip** (Studio Visit).  
  * **TALK Pillar**: Master the "Client Kickoff"—roleplaying discovery calls with professional authority.  
  * **Motion**: Create a cinematic 15-second product walkthrough video using **Jitter**.  
  * **Project**: **P1 Delivery**: Export a dev-ready Figma file and a data-backed Notion case study.

**Focus**: Enterprise SaaS, Corporate Politics, and Behavioral Logic.

  * **Project**: **P2 Setup**: Map the "Global Logic" and PRD for the **FinTech Platform**.

* **Week 6: Data Visualization & AI Studio**  
  * **Design**: Architect high-density data tables and interactive charts for financial tracking.  
  * **Figma Advanced**: Master **Figma Variables & Expressions** for logic-driven, dynamic prototyping.  
  * **AI Skills**: Use **Google AI Studio** to generate realistic CSV data sets for dashboard stress-testing.  
  * **Negotiation**: Master **Value-Based Pricing**—learning how to price projects based on ROI, not hours.  
  * **Project**: Design the "Master Dashboard" featuring 5+ complex, data-rich widgets.

  * **Psychology**: Apply the **Octalysis Framework** to create ethical retention loops and hooks.

  * **AI Simulation**: Use **AI Persona Agents** to simulate user testing and find logic friction points.  
  * **Project**: Design the "Investment Flow" with integrated Lottie micro-interactions.
